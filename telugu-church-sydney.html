Wherever you are in your journey with God, there's a seat for you here.

We are a congregation of Telugu-speaking families across Sydney — students, young professionals, and generations of families — who have made this city home while holding on to the faith and language of our own. Our services blend Telugu hymns, scripture, and prayer with the warmth of a shared table afterwards.

మీరు ఎక్కడ ఉన్నా, దేవుని ఎదుట మీకు ఒక స్థానం ఉంది.

New here? Here's what to expect

  • Language Service is conducted primarily in Telugu, with English available for youth and visitors.
  • Dress Come as you are — there's no dress code.
  • Children Sunday School runs alongside the main service for kids of all ages.
  • After service Stay back for tea, snacks, and conversation — this is often the best part.
